audi A1

The Audi A1 (internally designated Typ 8X) is a supermini car launched by Audi at the 2010 Geneva Motor Show. Sales of the initial three-door A1 model started in Germany in August 2010, with the United Kingdom following in November 2010.[2] A five-door version, called Sportback, was launched in November 2011, with sales starting in export markets during spring 2012
